Pokemon Fighting App

This program was originally made in c++ but translated into Javascript/React code to make an app.
The Pokemon Fighting App uses the PokemonAPI to retrieve Pokemon base health, attack, and defense along with other attributes,
then make the pokemon you choose to fight each other (with a sprite animation made with adobe photoshop) and tell you the winner, all damage dealt, received, and turns it takes to finish.
